  • Fair Practices: Transparent pricing and no manipulation.
  • Dispute Resolution: You can report issues to authorities.

‚ÄéUnregulated Brokers

‚Äé7.2 Setting Up a Trading Platform (MT4/MT5)

Once you choose a broker, you‚Äôll need a trading platform. The most common ones are MetaTrader 4 (MT4) and MetaTrader 5 (MT5).

Steps to Set Up MT4/MT5:

  1. Download the platform from your broker or the official MetaTrader site.
  2. Install and log in using your broker credentials.
  3. Explore key areas like Market Watch, Chart Window, and Terminal.
  4. Customize charts with indicators and timeframes.
  5. Save templates and enable one-click trading for convenience.

MT4 vs MT5

MT4: Best for forex trading, simple and reliable.
MT5: Offers advanced tools, faster execution, and more order types.

As a beginner, start with MT4. You can move to MT5 later when you gain experience.
